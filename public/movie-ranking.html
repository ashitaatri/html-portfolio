The Best Movies According to Ashita

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar (2014)

Christopher Nolan’s Interstellar captivates audiences with its mind-bending exploration of time, space,and human emotion. The film’s blend of scientific realism, stunning visuals,and emotional depth particularly the bond between a father and daughter resonates deeply.Hans Zimmer’s iconic score and the philosophical themes of survival and love make it a thought-provoking cinematic experience.


Turning Red (2022)

Turning Red is a heartwarming coming of age story that uses the metaphor of turning into a red panda to explore adolescence, identity,and family expectations. Its vibrant animation, cultural representation, and balance of humor and emotional depth appeal to a wide audience.Many love how it blends universal themes with a fresh and relatable narrative.


Now You See Me (2013)

Now You See Me offers an exciting blend of magic, crime, and clever twists. The film’s unique concept of magicians pulling off heists,alongside a talented ensemble cast, keeps viewers engaged and guessing throughout.Its fast-paced storytelling, dazzling visuals, and escapist fun make it a fan favorite for pure entertainment.
